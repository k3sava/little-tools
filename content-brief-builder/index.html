# Content Brief

**Title:** How to Choose the Right Business Phone System in 2026
**Primary Keyword:** business phone system
**Secondary Keywords:** VoIP for business, cloud phone system, business calling software
**Search Intent:** Commercial
**Content Type:** Comparison Article
**Word Count:** 2000-3000
**Tone:** Professional

## Target Audience
SMB operations managers evaluating phone systems for 10-200 person teams

**Pain Points:**
Existing system is expensive and hard to manage. Dropped calls. Poor mobile experience. No integrations with CRM.

## Outline
### Why your phone system matters
_Cost of missed calls, productivity impact_

### Key features to evaluate
_Call quality, mobile app, integrations, analytics, AI_

### Top 5 business phone systems compared
_Feature comparison table, pricing, pros/cons_

### How to evaluate for your team
_Decision framework, free trial checklist_

### Making the switch
_Migration steps, porting numbers, training_

## Key Points to Cover
Include real pricing (not just 'contact sales'). Address hybrid/remote work. Mention AI features as differentiator.

## Links
**Internal:** /features, /pricing, /integrations
**External:** G2 reviews, Gartner market guide

## CTA
**Primary:** Start free trial
**Placement:** After comparison table and at end of article
