About

RICE prioritizes ideas by (Reach × Impact × Confidence) / Effort. Higher score wins. Originally from Intercom.

Reach: users/qtr affected. Impact: 0.25–3. Confidence: 50–100%. Effort: person-months.

State auto-saves to localStorage. Import/export CSV to share with stakeholders.
